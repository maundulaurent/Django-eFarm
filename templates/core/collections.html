Understanding Our Collections

Our farm is organized in a hierarchical structure to help you find exactly what you need:

  • 1. Main Collections: Livestock, Produce, and Services - the top-level categories
  • 2. Categories: e.g., Cattle, Poultry, Vegetables - specific groups within each collection
  • 3. Subcategories: e.g., Dairy Cows, Broilers, Leafy Greens - more specific types
  • 4. Specific Items/Breeds: e.g., Holstein, Kale, Free-range Eggs - individual products

*Click on any category to drill down through the hierarchy. Each level filters to show more specific items.
